My interest in computers started when my Father’s Toshiba laptop’s screen began to flicker and eventually became inoperable. It fascinated me at a young age when the computer I presumed to be useless was plugged into a display and found a new lease on life. Since then I have been fascinated by the software and hardware that we so often think of as commodity devices.
